Cryptdate is a the product of one man's love for both cryptozoology and text-based adventures. In this case, maybe a little too much love. With five different monsters and routes to play, this game will have you catching not only the giggles but perhaps even feelings for some of history's most prolific cryptids. Such cryptids included are Mothman, the Lochness Monster, Count Dracula, Bigfoot, and for you dog lovers out there, a werewolf!

Will you fall into a trap or fall in love? Only time will tell once you start playing.
