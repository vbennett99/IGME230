I. Overview

CryptFit is a comedic and creative take on the traditional dress-up game that many people will remember throughout their childhoods.

II. Genre

CryptFit is a dress-up game.

III. Platform

CryptFit will be an in-browser game, able to be played in mobile but not necessarily fully supported. Mainly desktop.

IV. Story

Mothman is getting ready to go on a Cryptdate™ and needs your help to pick his outfit!

V. Aesthetics

There will be graphics to the game, 2D digital art. The game will be in full color with a simple background but with a fair amount of outfit choices. The player can choose to leave the game black text on white background or change to a dark theme with white text on a dark grey background. Text size will be moderate so there's no eye strain.

VI. Gameplay

On desktop the gameplay will mainly be mouse clicks on buttons but if it is to be on mobile it will be gesture/touch supported.

Mechanics

The player clicks on and drags different outfits onto Mothman.

Controls

  • Mouse
  • Touch and/or Gesture
